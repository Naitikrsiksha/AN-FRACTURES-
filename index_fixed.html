Our Story

AN Fractures & Physiotherapist Clinic has been serving patients since 2010 with a commitment to providing world-class orthopaedic and physiotherapy care at affordable prices.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with digital X-ray, advanced physiotherapy equipment, and an in-house pharmacy — all under one roof for your convenience.

We believe in holistic healing — treating not just the injury, but the whole person.
